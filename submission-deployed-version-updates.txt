Built and deployed MausamOG, a multi-page GenAI-powered monsoon preparedness and citizen assistance platform. The deployed version includes a personalized readiness dashboard, AI-generated monsoon preparedness plans, emergency checklist tracking, localized alert cards, Mapbox-based risk visualization, travel advisory generation, multilingual safety assistant, and local emergency resource discovery. It also includes login/signup with magic-link authentication, persisted user plans and checklist state, cached/rate-limited assistant interactions, and deterministic fallback responses so the demo remains usable when external AI or map services are unavailable. The app was implemented with Next.js App Router, Server Actions, TypeScript, Zod validation, Neon Postgres, Drizzle ORM, Upstash Redis, Gmail OAuth/email support, and modular service/repository layers for maintainability.
