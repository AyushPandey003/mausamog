Used Google Gemini API, mainly gemini-2.5-flash, inside the server-side AI flows. Gemini generates structured JSON monsoon preparedness plans from city, pincode, landmark, household, mobility, language, and weather-risk context. It also powers travel advisories and the multilingual citizen safety assistant for practical monsoon guidance. The app validates AI output with Zod and falls back to deterministic safety guidance if Gemini is unavailable. Used Google Stitch as the design inspiration source for the Sentinel Flux-style visual direction, including dashboard layout, alert presentation, assistant screens, authentication screens, and overall UI structure. OpenAI Codex/ChatGPT was also used during development to help generate, review, debug, and document the Next.js/TypeScript implementation and final submission text.
