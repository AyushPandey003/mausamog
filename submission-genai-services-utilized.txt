Used Google Gemini API, mainly gemini-2.5-flash, in the server-side AI flows. Gemini generates structured JSON monsoon preparedness plans from city, pincode, landmark, household, mobility, selected language, and weather-risk context. It also powers route-aware travel advisories and the multilingual citizen safety assistant for practical monsoon guidance. AI output is parsed, validated with Zod, capped where needed, and replaced with deterministic safety fallbacks if Gemini is unavailable or returns invalid content. Used Google Stitch as the design inspiration source for the Sentinel Flux-style visual direction, including dashboard layout, alert presentation, assistant screens, authentication screens, and overall UI structure. OpenAI Codex/ChatGPT was used during development to help generate, review, debug, localize with i18next, test, and document the Next.js/TypeScript implementation and submission copy.
